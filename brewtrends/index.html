Outlets

The Brew Trend-Loni Kalbhor, Pune

Nestled in the tranquil and idyllic lanes of Loni Kalbhor, we stumbled upon the perfect location for Brew Trend Cafe, and it was simply irresistible. In the winter of 2018, we proudly opened our doors to the world. Step inside, and you'll find a welcoming haven where warmth and laughter abound. A place designed for connecting with friends and family over great conversations, while savoring our classic menu in the embrace of an enchanting old-school ambiance. At Brew Trend Cafe, we invite you to experience the charm of simplicity and the joy of shared moments.

The Brew Trend-Destination Center, Magarpatta, Pune

Welcome to our one-of-a-kind café, nestled amidst the bustling tech haven! Here at The Brew Trend, we offer an extraordinary experience that sets us apart from the rest. As you step inside, you'll be greeted by an atmosphere that effortlessly blends modernity with coziness, creating a sanctuary where tech enthusiasts and creatives can unwind and recharge. From casual meetups to lively tech discussions, our space is designed to facilitate connections and inspire collaborations among our guests. Join us in this dynamic hub where innovation thrives, ideas flow freely, and moments of serenity are woven into the bustling tapestry of the tech world. At The Brew Trend, we believe in more than just serving coffee; we are crafting an experience that celebrates the spirit of innovation and human connection. Come and be part of the magic!
